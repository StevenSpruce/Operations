Cross ILR reporting

This service enables you to identify changes between two ILR submissions and assess their impact on funding.

You will be prompted to select an academic year, then a return period, and finally the ILR files to compare, which are filtered by that return period.

The funding period defaults to 12, but you can select any period from 1 to 12. The funding shown in the report is cumulative, up to and including the selected period.

Additional filters allow you to select sub-contract UKPRNs and Source of Funding.
