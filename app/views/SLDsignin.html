Submit Learner Data

This service is for organisations submitting, validating or viewing data.

For FE providers this includes:

  • Individualised Learner Records (ILR)
  • Earnings Adjustment Statements (EAS)
  • Funding Claims
  • Further Education Staff and Vacancy Data
  • Further Education Governor Data
  • 16-19 Student Estimate
  • employer identification
  • reports (including period end reports)

For devolved organisations this includes:

  • Skills Bootcamp Contracts
  • Funded Aims
  • Devolved Contracts (for adult skills)
  • period end reports
