Record FE Teacher FTE for 2024/25

Who to include in this figure

The figure should include the total Full-Time-Equivalent (FTE) for teachers employed on 20th January 2025 who were on:

  • Permanent contracts
  • Fixed-term contracts

How the prepopulated number is calculated

We calculate the sum of FTE for staff who meet all the following criteria:

  • Role: Their main role is listed as code 17 to 23 (teaching or teaching-related roles)
  • Contract: Their contract type is code 1 (Permanent) or 2 (Fixed-term)
  • Dates: Their employment started on or before 20 January 2025
  • Active Status: Their employment ended on or after 20 January 2025 (or is still ongoing)
