Record FE Teacher FTE for 2024/25

Who to include in this figure

The figure should include the total Full-Time-Equivalent (FTE) for teachers employed on 20th January 2025 who were on:

  • Permanent contracts
  • Fixed-term contracts

The prepopulated numbers for 2024/2025 are calculated using the logic below:

Sum of FTE
WHERE MainRole IN (17,18,19,20,21,22,23) 
AND ContractType IN (1, 2) 
AND EmploymentStartDate <= [ReferenceDate] 
AND (EmploymentEndDate >= [ReferenceDate] OR EmploymentEndDate IS NULL)

Note: The [ReferenceDate] is 20 January 2025.

Based on your 2024/25 FE Workforce Data Collection (FEWDC) return, your FTE was calculated as 125. Is this correct?
Enter the total Full-Time Equivalent (FTE), not the headcount.
For example, two staff members working 0.50 FTE each should be recorded as 1.00.
