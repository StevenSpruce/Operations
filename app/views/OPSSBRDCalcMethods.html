Skills Bootcamp calculation methods

Descriptions

Start Payment
The start payment is based on a 5 day qualifying period (and other conditions before they are recorded in the ILR).
Mid-Course Payment
None
Completion Payment
The completion payment is based on the completion of learning and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners).
Outcome Payment
The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners).
