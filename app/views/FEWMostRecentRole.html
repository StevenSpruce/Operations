How long did they work in their most recent role in the organisation?

Record the length of time the staff member worked in their most recent position, up to the end of July of the academic year. Where the answer falls between 1 and 20 years, only record full years worked. For example, record 3 years and 11 months worked as '1 to 3 years'.
