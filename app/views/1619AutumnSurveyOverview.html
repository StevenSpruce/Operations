Estimated 16-19 participation data collection

We need to know the estimate of your 16-19 student numbers ahead of the R04 collection return.

Currently, we do not have any indication of 16-19 student numbers until December when the R04 collection return closes.

There is an increasingly strong drive to understand 16-19 participation earlier in the academic year in order to:

  • help us track progress on the government's Opportunity Mission
  • monitor the likely financial position for the academic year
  • inform better policy decision making as necessary

Therefore, during R02 and again at R03, we are asking you to provide an estimate of your 16-19 student numbers for the current academic year.

This collection will not be used to make any decisions about your funding.

Calculating and submitting your estimate

An online form is available for you to submit your estimate.

Your estimate should include 16-19 students that:

  • will have at least one funding model 25 and source of funding 107 learning aims
  • will meet the criteria to count as a start for funding purposes (see related content)
  • will have an earliest start date on or before the 1 November 2025
  • will fall within one of these 4 funding line types:
    • 19+ Continuing Students (excluding EHCP)
    • 19-24 Students with an EHCP
    • 16-19 High Needs Students
    • 16-19 Students (excluding High Needs Students)

Your estimate should not include 14-16 direct funded students.
