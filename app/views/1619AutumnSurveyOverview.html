Academic year: 1 August 2025 to 31 July 2026

16-19 estimated participation

Currently, we do not have any indication of likely participation numbers in-year until December when R04 data become available. There is an increasingly strong drive to understand 16-19 participation earlier in the academic year, to help us track progress on the government's Opportunity Mission, monitor the likely financial position for the academic year, and make better decisions about directing funding as necessary. This survey could significantly help planning by giving us earlier insight into likely student numbers. The survey, while not compulsory, would need your internal sign-off in the usual way. We will not use the information to make any decisions about your funding. We will assess the data at an aggregate level, however, we would ask for returns to have the same level of rigour as a formal return.

Use this collection service to record and submit:

  • FE staff data
  • FE vacancy and recruitment data

Recording and submitting data

Data can be submitted using either of the following methods:

  • Online form service for staff and vacancy data collection
  • XML file upload service for staff data collection only

XML file data can be prepared and generated using our desktop application or your own system

The time it takes to complete the collection will depend on how many staff are recorded and which recording method is used.

Staff roles to record

Record staff data for:

  • senior leaders
  • managers
  • teachers
  • teaching support
  • administration staff

Do not record data for:

  • agency staff
  • self-employed or freelance staff
  • staff working for a provider through an intermediary, such as a personal service company
  • individuals in support roles which do not directly support learning, for example maintenance, cleaning and other ancillary roles

How we use data

When you use this service, we will collect personal information such as staff member names. Read about how we use personal information
