Who to include in this figure

The figure should include the total Full-Time-Equivalent (FTE) for staff members whose main role is teacher and were employed on 15th January 2024 who were on:

  • Permanent contracts
  • Fixed-term contracts
Enter the total Full-Time Equivalent (FTE), not the headcount.
For example, two staff members working 0.50 FTE each should be recorded as 123.00.
