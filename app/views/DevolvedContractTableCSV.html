FE Teacher FTE Snapshot Collection

This snapshot acts as a leading indicator for progress toward the 6,500 teacher recruitment target. It helps inform decision-making earlier in the academic year before the full FE Workforce Data Collection.
Who should complete this and who to include

Provider Scope: You should complete this if you are a:

  • General FE College (including Tertiary)
  • Sixth Form College
  • School-based provider (e.g., former sixth form colleges that converted have 16-19 academies)

Staff Definition: Your estimate should include the total Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) for teachers on permanent or fixed-term contracts.

Enter the total Full-Time Equivalent (FTE), not the headcount. For example, two staff members working 0.5 FTE each should be recorded as 1.0.
