Guidance on how to complete the contract data CSV file

There are 10 data fields to fill out for each course.

Skills Bootcamp name

Column A

Data entry
Enter the name of the Skills Bootcamp.
Additional guidance
100 character max length
Example
Name of Skills Bootcamp

Provider UKPRN

Column B

Data entry
Enter the UKPRN of the bootcamp provider.
Additional guidance
8 character integer
Example
Provider UKPRN
10003467
10005478

Your contract reference number

Column C

Data entry
Enter the your contract reference number (optional).
Additional guidance
This can be up to 50 characters. It is an optional field
Example
Learner Reference Number
(Up to 12 characters)
SBHGV12091
SBHGV1A132

Unique Learner Number

Column D

Data entry
Enter the Unique Learner Number that has been assigned to the learner.
This is 10 digits.
Additional guidance
This is held on the Learner Register, obtained from the Learner Record Service.
Example
Unique Learner Number
(10 digits)
9358348236
4125349648

Contract Reference Number associated with learner

Column E

Data entry
Enter the Contract Reference Number for the Skills Bootcamp course that the learner will be attending.
This can be up to 20 characters starting with one of the following: SB / SBD / SBA / XSB.
Additional guidance
Every learner should be linked to a Skills Bootcamp course.
Example
Contract Reference Number associated with learner
(Up to 20 characters starting with SB / SBA / SBD / XSB)
SBD-1116-317
XSB-90001234-A000001

Has learner consented to be contacted for research purposes?

Column F

Data entry

Enter only one of the following options:

  • Yes
  • No
Additional guidance
This would involve the learner being contacted by third party research contractors to participate in interviews or surveys to gain their feedback.
Example
Has learner consented to be contacted for research purposes?
(Yes/No)
No
Yes

Learner's main work industry before applying to Skills Bootcamp

Column G

Data entry

You need to select the closest match using only one of the following options from the drop-down menu in the Excel template:

  • Agriculture/environmental and animal care
  • Business and administration
  • Care services
  • Catering and hospitality
  • Construction and the built environment
  • Creative and design
  • Digital
  • Education and early years
  • Engineering and manufacturing
  • Hair and beauty
  • Health and science
  • Legal/finance and accounting
  • Protective services
  • Sales/marketing and procurement
  • Transport and logistics
  • Extended period of unemployment
Additional guidance
If you are unsure, you can use this website to understand the different options in more detail.
Example
Learner's main work industry before applying to Skills Bootcamp
(Select from drop-down list)
Extended period of unemployment
Engineering and manufacturing

If employed: Name of learner's current employer

Column G

Data entry

If the learner is employed, enter the employer name as accurately as possible.

If the learner is unemployed or self-employed, enter:

  • Unemployed
  • Self-employed
Additional guidance
If their employer is not engaging with Skills Bootcamp, the learner can choose whether they wish to share this information.
Example
If employed: Name of learner's current employer
Unemployed
John Lewis
Self-employed

If co-funded: Name of learner's co-funder

Column I

Data entry

If the learner is co-funded, enter the name of the employer who is providing the co-funding as accurately as possible.

If the learner is not co-funded, enter:

  • Not applicable
Additional guidance
N/A
Example
If co-funded: Name of learner's co-funder
Royal Mail
Not applicable
Nationwide

Name of employer offering a guaranteed interview to learner

Column J

Data entry

Enter the name of the employer as accurately as possible.

If the learner is co-funded or self-employed, enter:

  • Not applicable
Additional guidance
This information may not be known at the start of the Skills Bootcamp. Complete this data field as soon as it is known.
Example
Name of employer offering a guaranteed interview to learner
Tesco
Deloitte
Not applicable

Skills Bootcamp completion: Learner's employment status

Column K

Data entry

You need to select only one of the following options from the drop-down menu in the Excel template:

  • No outcome/no change
  • Continuing in current employment with new responsibilities
  • New role with current employer
  • New full or part-time employment
  • New employment on an apprenticeship
  • Becoming or continuing to be self-employed with new opportunities
  • New full or part-time employment
  • Not applicable
Additional guidance
This information is only required after the learner completes the Skills Bootcamp course. Complete this data field once you have received a completion update from the learner.
Example
Skills Bootcamp completion: Learner's employment status
New full or part time employment
Not applicable
Continuing in current employment with new responsibilities
No outcome/no change
