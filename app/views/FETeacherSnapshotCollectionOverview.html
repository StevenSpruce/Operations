Estimated FE Teacher FTE

We need to know the estimate of your teacher Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) numbers ahead of the full Further Education Workforce Data Collection (FEWDC).

Currently, we do not have an indication of teacher workforce numbers until later in the year when the FEWDC return closes.

There is an increasingly strong drive to understand workforce numbers earlier in the academic year in order to:

  • act as a leading indicator for progress toward the 6,500 teacher recruitment target
  • provide basic statistics on how teacher numbers are changing throughout the academic year
  • inform decision making earlier in the academic year

Therefore, between Monday 2 February and Monday 16 February, we are asking you to provide a snapshot of your teacher FTE for the current academic year.

Calculating and submitting your estimate

An online form is available for you to submit your estimate.

Your estimate should include FTE for staff that:

  • are employed as teachers
  • are on permanent or fixed-term contracts
  • were employed on the snapshot date of 19 January

This collection applies to:

  • General FE Colleges (incl. Tertiary)
  • Sixth Form Colleges
  • School-based providers (e.g. converted 16-19 academies)

Your estimate should not include staff from Independent Training Providers (ITPs), Special Post-16 institutions, or Local Authority providers.
