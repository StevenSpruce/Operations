Academic year: 1 August 2025 to 31 July 2026

Teacher FTE Snapshot Collection

We are collecting this snapshot to track teacher numbers earlier in the academic year. This data will act as a leading indicator for progress toward the 6,500 teacher recruitment target and help inform decision-making before the full FE Workforce Data Collection (FEWDC).

Who should complete this and who to include

Which providers are in scope?

This collection is required for:

  • General FE Colleges (including Tertiary)
  • Sixth Form Colleges
  • School-based providers (e.g. ex-Sixth form colleges converted to 16-19 academies)

Do not complete this if you are an Independent Training Provider (ITP), Special Post-16 institution, or Local Authority provider.

Staff definition
