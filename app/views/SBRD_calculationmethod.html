Calculation methods
Code Start payment Mid-course payment Completion payment Outcome payment Employer co-funding
SBC01 The start payment is based on a 5 day qualifying period (and other conditions before they are recorded in the ILR) None The completion payment is based on the completion of learning and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
SBC03 The start payment is based on a 14 day qualifying period (and other conditions before they are recorded in the ILR) None The completion payment is based on the completion of learning and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
HGV01 The start payment is based on evidence that the learner has an HGV provisional licence (and other conditions before they are recorded in the ILR) The mid-course payment is based on the completion of learning and the CPC Part 3b test taken The completion payment is based on passing the CPC Part 3b and the CPC Part 4 and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
HGV02 None The mid-course payment is based on the completion of learning The completion payment is based on passing the CPC Part 3b and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
HGV03 None The mid-course payment is based on the completion of learning The completion payment is based on passing the ADR test and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
HGV04 None The mid-course payment is based on the completion of learning The completion payment is based on passing the ADR test and the PDP test and an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
HGV05 None The mid-course payment is based on the completion of learning The completion payment is based on evidence that the driver's CPC has been updated and has an offer of an interview (or alternatives for employed/ self-employed learners) The outcome payment is based on the commencement of a job or a role which utilises skills gained from the bootcamp (or alternatives for self-employed learners) The employer co-funding percentage is 10% for small/medium employers (less than 250 employees) and 30% for other employers
