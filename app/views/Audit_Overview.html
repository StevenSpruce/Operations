Provider Market Oversight: Assurance

This service is used to record the details of the testing of the following main substantive samples:

  • Learners funded within the Adult Skills Fund models FM35 and FM38
  • Apprentices funded within the new apprenticeship funding system from 1 May 2017, within funding model FM36
  • 16 to 19 learners (excluding apprentices) funded within the 16 to 19 funding model FM25
    • includes learners for providers without a 16 to 19 (excl. apprenticeships) funding agreement
    • includes learners enrolled on T Level programmes
  • Learners funded by Advanced Learner Loans and Loans Bursary Fund
