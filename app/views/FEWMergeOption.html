Record FE Teacher FTE for 2023/24

Who to include and how this figure is calculated

The figure should include the total Full-Time-Equivalent (FTE) for staff members whose main role is teacher and were employed on 15 January 2024 who were on:

  • Permanent contracts
  • Fixed-term contracts

Sum of FTE
Where MainRole = (17,18,19,20,21,22,23)
And ContractType = (1 or 2)
And EmploymentStartDate <= [15 January 2024]
And (EmploymentEndDate >= [15 January 2024] or EmploymentEndDate is null)
